# Legal, Privacy, Insurance, and Risk File

## Purpose

Keep professional advice, obligations, renewals, approvals, and unresolved risks organized as business assets. This folder should not be used to store routine client files.

## Maintain These Registers

### Professional Advisor Register

| Area | Advisor | Firm | Engagement scope | Last review | Next review | Key deliverable | Owner |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|

Cover corporate, tax, contracts, privacy, employment, intellectual property, insurance, franchising, and sale transactions.

### Obligation and Renewal Register

| Requirement | Applies to | Authority or contract | Responsible owner | Evidence | Renewal/review date | Status |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|

Include business registrations, licences, insurance, policies, contracts, training, vendor reviews, privacy reviews, trademark deadlines, and franchise documents where applicable.

### Risk Register

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Prevention | Contingency | Trigger | Owner | Review date | Status |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|

### Claims and Incidents Register

Use restricted access. Keep objective facts, professional advice, notifications, corrective actions, insurance communications, and closure evidence. Do not copy sensitive information into general management reports.

## Expansion Review Rule

Obtain fresh professional review before:

- Hiring or materially changing worker arrangements
- Opening a location
- Expanding outside the approved area
- Adding a client portal or sensitive-information vendor
- Introducing artificial intelligence involving client information
- Holding client funds
- Using referral commissions
- Licensing the brand or method
- Offering a franchise
- Taking investment
- Selling shares or assets
- Transferring client or employee information during a transaction

## Record Standard

For each professional decision, preserve:

- Question asked
- Facts provided
- Advice or deliverable received
- Decision-maker
- Decision
- Actions required
- Owner and due date
- Evidence of completion
- Next review trigger
