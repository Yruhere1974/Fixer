# Records and Evidence Index

## Purpose

Store proof that the business system is followed and works. Manuals describe intended operations; evidence demonstrates actual operations.

## Evidence Categories

- Approved policies, procedures, and version history
- Training and competency results
- Fictional-client and tabletop test results
- Founder-absence test results
- Location-readiness and opening approvals
- Quality-review results
- Provider-verification performance
- Aggregated client and service measures
- Privacy, incident, complaint, and corrective-action evidence
- Access reviews and vendor assessments
- Financial scorecards and reconciliations
- Expansion and franchise gate decisions
- Professional reviews and completion evidence
- Business-continuity and recovery tests
- Sale-readiness review results

## Rules

- Use aggregated, de-identified, or fictional evidence when individual identity is unnecessary.
- Do not copy raw client health information into general business evidence.
- Restrict complaint, incident, employee, and legal evidence appropriately.
- Connect every material finding to an owner, deadline, correction, and retest.
- Preserve source evidence supporting financial or performance claims.
- Record failed tests; do not keep only favourable results.
- Apply approved retention and legal-hold rules.
