# When Health and Wellness Gets Complicated, You Don’t Have to Manage It Alone

## Personal health and wellness coordination in Kelowna

Finding the right support should not feel like a full-time job. Yet for many people, improving their health—or helping an aging parent—means juggling appointments, researching providers, arranging transportation, comparing services, keeping family members informed, and trying to understand what needs to happen next.

That is where I come in.

I am a **Kelowna Health & Wellness Navigator**: a reliable local problem-solver who helps individuals and families turn complicated situations into clear, manageable action plans. I handle the research, organization, coordination, and follow-through so you can spend less time chasing details and more time focusing on your well-being.

### How I can help

- Clarify your goals, priorities, budget, and immediate needs
- Research appropriate local professionals, programs, and practical supports
- Check the credentials of regulated health professionals
- Coordinate appointments, transportation, meals, fitness, home support, and other logistics
- Prepare useful questions for upcoming appointments
- Attend appointments as a support person or note-taker, with permission
- Organize information, contacts, deadlines, and next steps in one clear plan
- Keep approved family members updated
- Follow up to make sure important details do not fall through the cracks

### Who this service is for

My service is especially helpful for:

- Busy adults who feel overwhelmed by health and wellness decisions
- Families coordinating care for an aging parent in Kelowna
- Adult children who live outside the Okanagan and need a trusted local contact
- People preparing for or recovering from a procedure
- Individuals building a more organized, sustainable wellness routine
- Anyone who needs practical support navigating local services

### A trusted guide—not another medical provider

I do not diagnose conditions, provide treatment, prescribe medication, or replace your doctor or other qualified health professionals. My role is to help you find appropriate support, ask better questions, manage the logistics, and carry your plan forward. Your privacy, independence, and right to make your own decisions always come first.
